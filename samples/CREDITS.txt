Acoustic guitar (steel) and nylon-guitar samples used in Drafthaus Lite are
from the FluidR3_GM soundfont, generated by the gleitz/midi-js-soundfonts
project (https://github.com/gleitz/midi-js-soundfonts).

FluidR3_GM is released under the Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 license
(https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/). Original soundfont by Frank Wen.

Drafthaus Lite uses:
- guitar/   = acoustic_guitar_steel  (acoustic guitar voice)
- ukulele/  = acoustic_guitar_nylon  (nylon strings, played in the uke register)
